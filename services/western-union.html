What do I need to send a money transfer?▾

You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID and the recipient's details including their full name, city, and country. You'll also need cash to cover the transfer amount and any applicable fees.

How long does a money transfer take?▾

Most Western Union money transfers are available for pickup within minutes, though some may take longer depending on the destination country, payment method, and regulatory requirements.

What is the maximum money order amount?▾

You can purchase individual money orders up to $1,000 each. Money orders must be purchased with cash and are a secure, widely accepted form of payment.

Can I track my money transfer?▾

Yes. When you send a transfer, you'll receive a Money Transfer Control Number (MTCN). Use this number to track your transfer at westernunion.com, through the Western Union app, or by calling Western Union directly.

What bills can I pay through Western Union?▾

You can pay a wide range of bills including mortgage, auto loans, credit cards, insurance, utilities, government bills, and more from participating billers across the United States.

What is Stage & Pay?▾

Stage & Pay lets you start your money transfer on the Western Union app or website, then select "Pay in Store" at checkout. Visit any Cash in Flash location to complete your payment in cash — it's the best of both worlds.
