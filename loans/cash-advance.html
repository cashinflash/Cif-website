How much can I get with a cash advance?▾

Cash in Flash cash advances range from $100 to $255, depending on your income and California state regulations.

What are the fees for a cash advance?▾

Fees are based on your advance amount. In California, the maximum fee is $15 for every $100 borrowed. Your exact fee will be clearly disclosed before you sign any agreement, so there are no surprises.

How quickly will I receive my cash advance?▾

If approved, you can often receive your funds the same day. In-store applicants may receive cash immediately. Online applicants typically receive a direct deposit by the next business day.

Can I get a cash advance with bad credit?▾

Yes. Cash advance applications are reviewed using multiple factors — not just your credit score. Your income, employment history, and ability to repay are all considered during the approval process.

How do I repay my cash advance?▾

Your cash advance is typically due on your next payday. You can repay in-store with cash or debit, or set up automatic repayment from your bank account. We'll review all your options before you finalize your agreement.

Can I apply for a cash advance online?▾

Absolutely. You can apply online from your phone or computer in about 5 minutes. Once approved, funds are deposited directly into your bank account.

What is the difference between a cash advance and a payday loan?▾

A cash advance and a payday loan are very similar — both are short-term loans designed to provide quick access to funds between paychecks. At Cash in Flash, both options offer the same loan amounts ($100 to $255), the same fast approval process, and the same repayment terms.

Is a cash advance safe?▾

Yes. Cash in Flash is a licensed lender in California. All fees and terms are disclosed upfront before you sign anything. We follow all state regulations and never charge hidden fees.
