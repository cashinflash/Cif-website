How much can I borrow with a payday loan?▾

Cash in Flash payday loans range from $100 to $255, depending on your income and the state regulations in California.

What are the fees for a payday loan?▾

Fees vary based on your loan amount. In California, the maximum fee is $15 for every $100 borrowed. Your exact fee will be disclosed before you sign any agreement, so there are no surprises.

How quickly will I receive my funds?▾

If approved, you can often receive your funds the same day. In-store applicants may receive cash immediately. Online applicants typically get direct deposit by the next business day.

Can I apply with bad credit?▾

Yes. Payday loan applications are reviewed using multiple factors — not just your credit score. Your income, employment history, and ability to repay are also considered during the approval process.

How do I repay my payday loan?▾

Your payday loan is typically due on your next payday. You can repay in-store with cash or debit, or set up automatic repayment from your bank account. We'll go over all your options before you finalize your loan.

Can I get a payday loan online?▾

Absolutely. You can apply online from your phone or computer in about 5 minutes. Once approved, funds are deposited directly into your bank account.
