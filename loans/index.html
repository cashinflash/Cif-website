What types of loans does Cash in Flash offer?▾

Cash in Flash offers payday loans from $100 to $255 and cash advances. Both are designed to help you cover short-term expenses quickly and easily.

How much can I borrow?▾

You can borrow from $100 to $255, depending on your income and California state regulations. The exact amount will be determined during the application process.

How do I apply for a loan?▾

You can apply online in about 5 minutes, call us, or visit one of our California store locations. You'll need a valid government ID, proof of income, an active bank account, and your Social Security number.

Do I need good credit to apply?▾

No. We look at more than just your credit score. Your income, employment history, and ability to repay are also considered. Applying will not affect your FICO score.

How quickly will I receive my funds?▾

If approved, you can often receive your funds the same day. In-store applicants may receive cash immediately. Online applicants typically get direct deposit by the next business day.

How do I repay my loan?▾

Your loan is typically due on your next payday. You can repay in-store with cash or debit, or set up automatic repayment from your bank account. We'll go over all your options before you finalize your loan.
