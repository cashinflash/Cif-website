Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making our website accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

Cash in Flash (Dhan Corporation) is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide a positive experience for everyone and are continually improving our website to ensure it meets or exceeds applicable accessibility standards.

Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines are developed by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) and provide a framework for making web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including:

  • Visual impairments (blindness, low vision, color blindness)
  • Hearing impairments
  • Motor disabilities
  • Cognitive and learning disabilities

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper HTML elements (headings, lists, landmarks) to convey structure and meaning
  • Alt Text: Images include descriptive alternative text for screen reader users
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements can be accessed using a keyboard alone
  • Color Contrast: We maintain sufficient color contrast ratios for text and interactive elements
  • Resizable Text: Text can be resized using browser zoom without loss of content or functionality
  • Descriptive Links: Link text is descriptive and meaningful out of context
  • Form Labels: Form inputs include associated labels for screen reader compatibility
  • ARIA Attributes: We use ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) attributes where appropriate to enhance accessibility

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with commonly used assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvement. Our efforts include:

  • Regular review and testing of our website for accessibility barriers
  • Training our team on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating accessibility into our design and development processes
  • Promptly addressing accessibility issues as they are identified

Alternative Access

If you are unable to access any content or features on our website due to a disability, we are happy to assist you through alternative means. You can:
